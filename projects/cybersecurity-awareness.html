Cybersecurity Awareness Program

A workplace-focused audit and awareness program addressing common human and operational security risks.

Project Outcome

Completed

Reviewed a simulated organisation's security culture, assessed the business impact of observed behaviours, prioritised risks and developed practical awareness recommendations.

Deliverables

✓ Staff awareness and training content

✓ Review process for emerging threats and program improvement

Key Learning

Effective cybersecurity depends on people, process and technology. Controls are stronger when staff understand why they matter and receive clear, practical guidance.
